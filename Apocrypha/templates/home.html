Welcome to Apocrypha

Apocrypha is a global, decentralized, ethics-driven startup ecosystem. We are not a charity or a fund—we are a business creation engine where fairness, collaboration, and long-term thinking are competitive advantages. Here, being ethical and being profitable are not opposites. We let startups operate as they will, with only a few simple beliefs: help others, especially your fellow startups; invest in each other and share equity; avoid underhanded tricks; and focus on long-term relationships over short-term profits.

  • Mission: Democratize startup creation and participation. Encourage moral behavior and responsibility through empathy-driven, gamified ethics—not centralized control.
  • ASF (Agentic Startup Factory): An open-source AI agent that helps refine ideas, form teams, allocate tasks, and suggest equity splits—always guided by human review and ethical oversight.
  • Equity & Governance: Earn equity by contributing. All proposals are peer-reviewed, with an ethics board for oversight. Power rotates, never accumulates. Even core contributors are guiding elders, not permanent leaders.
  • Community Advantage: Our community provides unlimited beta testers, market surveys, and a built-in audience for every launch. This means better products, faster feedback, and a proven customer base—exactly what VCs want to see.
  • Investment Model: Systematic investment pools let anyone invest without burden, helping launch dozens of lean, AI-powered businesses every year. Your contribution helps build a fair, profitable, and sustainable ecosystem.
  • Meme & Morality: Our evolving "Apocrypha-The Divinity" NFT and karma system gamify empathy and ethical action, building a culture of shared responsibility and inspiration.

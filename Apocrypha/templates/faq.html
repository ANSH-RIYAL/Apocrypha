Frequently Asked Questions

What is Apocrypha?
Apocrypha is a global, decentralized, ethics-driven startup ecosystem. It empowers people—especially in underdeveloped regions—to build startups, earn equity, and participate in a culture of principled, profitable business creation.
How can I contribute?
Join as a coder, idea contributor, labor contributor, or supporter. Every contribution is recognized through our equity and karma systems. See the Join page for more.
How does investment work?
Investors support individual startups, not Apocrypha itself. Funds are pooled and distributed transparently. Investment does not grant governance power.
What is the Agentic Startup Factory (ASF)?
The ASF is an open-source AI agent that helps refine ideas, form teams, allocate tasks, and suggest equity splits. All decisions are peer-reviewed and guided by ethical oversight.
How does the karma system work?
Karma points are earned by contributing, voting, and engaging ethically. Karma determines eligibility for the rotating ethics board and influences community decisions.
What is the Apocrypha-The Divinity NFT?
It is a symbolic, community-evolving NFT that represents our shared values and ethical culture. It is not about hierarchy or dogma, but about collective imagination and accountability.
